The Death of Makise Kurisu

The inciting incident

One a fairly routine, yet unsuccessful, mission looking for allies I heard a scream. Upon investigating the situation, I stumbled upon the body of published neuroscientist Makise Kurisu. I was in shock as I had only just met her moment earlier. Upon notifying others that they should call emergency medical units, I fled the scene and sent a text notifying my right-hand man, Daru, of the event. Daru, unbeknownst to me, was testing the Phone Wave (Real name subject to change) at the exact same time...

The Living Dead!!!

An uncanny encounter

Later that same day, while attending a school event, I encountered Makise Kurisu once more. This could not be! I was convinced that she had risen from the dead! After a regrettable encounter, she eventually came over to the lab to see what had driven to me to accost her. This is when we confirmed that the Phone Wave (Real name subject to change) could be used to send small text messages into the past!

Our dear, sweet, naive Kurisutina denied it at first, but eventually joined our lab to experiment with the device to see what, if anything, we could potentially change.

Reading Steiner

The Observer of Time

Our testing revealed one thing for certain, that I was the only one who remembered the way things were before we send text messages into the past. Such as the satellite crashed into the building where Makise Kurisu had been murdered.

I dubbed this ability Reading Steiner as the threshold to observe time itself is known to all as Steins;Gate and by observing the way it changed I was reading the fabric of time itself!

This power would not go unnoticed, however, and we soon became the targets of SERN. In our dimension, they were formed with the express intent of creating black holes as a way to traverse physically through time. Our discovery was both a danger and a major leap forward for them. Before we could reveal our latest iteration of the Phone Wave (Real name subject to change), the Time Leap Machine, disaster struck.

How I arrived here today

A cycle of death

SERN's agents, known as Rounders, broke into our lab with intention to collect the Phone Wave (Real name subject to change), the Time Leap Machine, and everyone involved with it's development. The leader of the team, double agent Kiryu Moeka, deemed Lab Member 002, Shina Mayuri, unnecessary to be collected for the mission and killed her.

In great sorrow and desperation, I was able to use the Time Leap Machine to before these events happened. However, no matter how many times I tried Mayuri would still die, as if it were an event pre-determined by fate itself.

It is through these experiments that I eventually ended up here today. The machine malfunctioned in an unexpected way and I was thrust from this never-ending spiral into this reality.
