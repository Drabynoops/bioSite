Higher Education

Prior to my being thrust into this dimension, I studied engineering at Tokyo Denki University. Though during my time my assistant, I gained an interest in nueroscience.

However, for the time being, I am continuing along a slightly different path. My dimensional counterpart also seemed to be an engineer. In fact, I would expect nothing else from any version of myself! As such, I am pursuing skills in programming and development. This biography site is part of this.

What a convenient project! I can make this site to practice the skills I need to survive in this strange world and it gives another layer of protection from those that might be trying to track me down. Who would believe that an interdimensional mad scientist would choose to reveal himself in the form of a simple college web development assignment?
